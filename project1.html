My top 3 movies of all time

Fighting club

*Fight Club* is a gritty and provocative film that explores the psychological toll of modern consumerism and identity loss. Edward Norton and Brad Pitt deliver intense performances in a story filled with twists and dark philosophy. Its raw narrative and shocking ending leave a lasting impact, making it a cult classic.

3 idiots

*3 Idiots* is a heartwarming and thought-provoking film that challenges the rigid Indian education system through humor and emotion. Aamir Khan leads a stellar cast in a story about friendship, passion, and finding one's true path. Its mix of inspiration, satire, and memorable moments makes it a modern classic.

Baahubali

*Baahubali* is an epic saga of power, betrayal, and legacy, brought to life with grand visuals and stunning action sequences. Director S.S. Rajamouli crafts a mythical world anchored by Prabhas's heroic performance. Its scale, storytelling, and "Why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?" mystery made it a cultural phenomenon.
